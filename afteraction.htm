After Action Report!

Combat Mission Shockforce 2: NATO Canadian Club SF2


Initial Assessment

The Canadian Army has been assigned a complex assault mission. They are tasked with securing both a bomb factory advanced enemy stronghold. Due to limited planning, poor logistics, and time delays the Canadian army is forced to attack with both limited resources and fire-support.


Tactics and Strategies

The initial Assault force were ambushed as they approached the bomb factory they suffered minimal several casualties However, due to our tactical approach our troops were able to find cover and return fire. They easily squashed the ambush. After securing the factory the BTRs were able to begin their assault. Mortars suppress the stronghold and farm. Our engineers were able to blast their way into the buildings and secure the last objective.
